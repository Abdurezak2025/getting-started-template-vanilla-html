## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, password
- **Profile Information:** Farm details, crop types, location, business information
- **Financial Data:** Banking information, transaction history, payment details
- **Documents:** Contracts, certifications, permits

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, OS, browser, unique identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, actions taken, timestamps
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates (only with your permission)
- **Sensor Data:** Weather, soil moisture, temperature readings (from IoT sensors)

### 2.3 Third-Party Data Sources
- **Weather Data:** OpenMeteo API (free weather service)
- **Market Data:** Public agribusiness indices and commodity prices

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- User account management and authentication

- Processing financial transactions
- Supply chain and logistics management
- AI diagnostics and crop recommendations
- GIS mapping and IoT sensor integration
- Improving app functionality and performance
- Compliance with legal obligations
- Customer support and communication

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
- HTTPS/TLS for data transmission
- Service Worker caching for offline security
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- ISO 27001 compliance
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) support

**Note:** No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we use best practices, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Offline Functionality

The App includes offline capability via Service Worker. Data stored locally on your device is not controlled by us. You can clear this data through your browser settings:
- Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Clear browsing data
- Safari: Develop > Empty Caches
- Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
